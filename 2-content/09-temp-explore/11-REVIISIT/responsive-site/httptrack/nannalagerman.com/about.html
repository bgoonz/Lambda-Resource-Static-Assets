As a small award-winning creative studio we specialize in elevating spaces and interiors, bringing thought, relevance and style to forward thinking environments. 

We founded the studio out of a passion to add value, seek pleasure in spaces and reconnect people with style, insight and new experiences and we believe in sharing and embracing the yet unexplored.we are based in Stockholm and have more than 25 years of experience helping brands
and people to achieve more.

we are  designing various interiors, mainly commercial spaces and high-end residential homes, managing everything from bespoke interior solutions
 and tailor-made concepts for all interior areas including kitchens, bathrooms, furniture, lighting, colors and textiles.  we also curate art selection and the final styling of the smaller objects that complete a space.  Additionally, we create set design that consistently support the brand
and editorial story for international interior brands and magazines.

Working as an independent creative
in close collaboration with dedicated set-up teams, we act as both a strategic and creative resource to my clients
at every step of the process, aligning inspiration, business objectiveness
and story-telling into powerful and clear experiences. My network is flexible and covers from project management and architectural services to craftsmanship and purchasing channels, all the way down to photo shoots and styling. Through many years of extensive experience, I have the expertise and resources required to execute more or less any kind of project.

PHILOSOPHY

I´m a creator of interior worlds since I was a child. I early found a purpose to escape into spaces that I could transform into good feelings and still today it´s the way I approach spaces. To me it´s all about the experience of the interior and I´m a believer that what you choose to surround yourself with affects your well-being and your body.

In rough times we need a space that we prioritize, which gives us calm and a sense of wanting to escape. My perspective as an interior designer has always been very cinematic and my job has always been to help people escape.

For us the purpose of interior design is not necessarily to reflect the spirit of time, but to respond to it and create solutions for the fast growing every day. we believe it will be more important than ever to be aware of the rooms and spaces we put into the world and for how a daily life will look like.
